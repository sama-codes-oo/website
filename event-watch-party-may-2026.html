Bad Buddy Movie Night

Watch Party May 17, 2026 Mumbai Recap

About this event

Our May watch party brought the crew together for a fan-favourite screening of Bad Buddy, complete with themed keepsakes, live commentary, matching bracelets, and a cozy fan-first atmosphere. We watched episodes 11 and 12 together, celebrated the finale, and made memory cards to take home.

The room filled up fast, the reactions were everything, and we left with full hearts and a few new friendships. This is exactly the kind of night BLxKpopCrew was made for.
