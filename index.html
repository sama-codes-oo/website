Format

Built for fans, creators, and first-timers.

You do not need to know everyone already. Each experience has a host, a simple plan, and a clear opt-in approach to photos, introductions, content, and discussion.

Before

RSVP and event notes

Event posts include venue area, timing, themes, watchlist details, age rating, and any content notes to be aware of.

During

Host-led, fan-friendly flow

We keep introductions optional, separate spoiler zones from general chat, and make space for quiet participation.

Community

What the crew makes room for.

A fandom space works when people feel respected, excited, and free to celebrate what they love.

Respect

Queer-friendly and judgement-free

All fans are welcome. We ask members to respect pronouns, boundaries, ships, comfort levels, and lived experiences.

Privacy

Photos only with consent

Group photos are opt-in. No face tags, reposts, or public stories without permission from the people shown.
