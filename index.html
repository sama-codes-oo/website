Format

Built for fans, introverts, and first-timers.

You do not need to know everyone already. Each meetup has a host, a simple plan, and a clear opt-in approach to photos, introductions, and discussion.

Before

RSVP and content notes

Event posts include venue area, timing, watchlist, language/subtitle info, age rating, and any themes to be aware of.

During

Host-led, spoiler-safe flow

We keep introductions optional, separate spoiler zones from general chat, and make space for quiet participation.

Community

What we make room for.

A fandom space works when people feel respected. These are the defaults we build every meetup around.

Respect

Queer-friendly and judgement-free

All fans are welcome. We ask members to respect pronouns, boundaries, ships, comfort levels, and lived experiences.

Privacy

Photos only with consent

Group photos are opt-in. No face tags, reposts, or public stories without permission from the people shown.

Fandom

Recommendations welcome

Bring your watchlist, fanart, playlists, lightsticks, photocards, zines, or theories. Enthusiasm is the point.
